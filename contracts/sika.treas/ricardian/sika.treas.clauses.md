<h1 class="clause">settlement-disclosure</h1>

Treasury settlement actions move value between market ledgers, reserves, and user wallets under parameters set by governance. FX conversions use attested rates when signed oracle push is enabled.

<h1 class="clause">governance-only</h1>

Actions marked for system, oracle, or governance authority alter network-wide economics. They do not originate from consumer wallets unless explicitly authorized as a multisig or BP operation.
